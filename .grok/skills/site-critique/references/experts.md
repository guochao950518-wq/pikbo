## Namya @namyakhann (Supafast) — B2B/AI SaaS 落地页

**Clients cited publicly:** You.com, 11x, OpusClip, etc.

**Core ideas:**

- 99% 设计只追求好看；转化靠结构。
- 失败清单：关于你的标题、列功能不写结果、Learn more CTA、首屏无社证、什么都讲。
- 公式：Nav+CTA → 利益标题 → 副标题解释 → CTA → 社证 → 产品图/视频。
- Copy is king；Grand Slam Offer 降低门槛；How it works 3–5 步；Pricing 可见。
- 理想买家会读长文/看 demo — 为买家写，不为「路人短注意力」阉割信息。
- 高转化文案可来自销售通话原话（异议 → FAQ；问题语言 → 标题）。

## Oliver Kenyon — post-click / landing structure

**Structure:** Attention → Transformation (3 steps) → Interest → Desire → FAQ → Action.

Each section has fixed jobs (value prop, proof, benefits, testimonials, objections, final CTA).

## Indie conversion signals (e.g. Om Patel & peers)

- 砍掉无意义动画/fade 若挡理解。
- 付费墙前必须让用户 **看见付费后得到什么**。
- 堆真实结果与 testimonials；展示用户用产品做出的东西。
- people buy when they can see the thing working.

## Growthpanda / generic SaaS CRO chorus

- Page is the salesperson; 5 seconds to get what / who / why me.
- Confusion loses more than competitors.
- Plain language; one ICP; outcome not supercharge-speak.
- Fix page before scaling ads.
